Wedding details

Thank you for joining our little celebration! Here are some details to help you plan your trip. If you have any questions, please e-mail us at wedding@sarahandcurt.us.

Wedding day and time

Saturday, June 20, 2015 at 6 p.m.

The wedding ferry departs at 4 p.m. sharp. Be there on time or you'll literally miss the boat! (See details below.)

Getting to the wedding

Greyfield Inn, our wedding venue, is located on Cumberland Island, a wild and wonderful place. (more information)

The Inn is only accessible by ferry boat, which departs from downtown Fernandia Beach, Florida, on Amelia Island. Do not follow Google Maps to Cumberland Island via St. Mary's, Georgia. That will lead you to the wrong boat.

The ferry carries passengers and luggage only (no cars).

The ferry departs from Fernandina beach at 4 p.m. After the wedding, the ferry will depart Greyfield at 11 p.m.

The nearest major airport is Jacksonville International Airport, about an hour's-drive from Fernandina Beach.

Where to stay on Amelia Island

We have a block of rooms reserved at the Hampton Inn & Suites in downtown Fernandina Beach, Florida, from Thursday, June 18, to Monday, June 22.

Depending on the room, the rate is $129, $139 or $149 per night. To receive the block rate, make reservations no later than May 20, 2015. Call 904-491-4911 and use the secret password “Blizzard-Merrill Wedding.”

If you wish to turn your trip into a longer vacation, there are a number of lodging options on Amelia Island. Sarah's family has enjoyed many vacations at the Amelia Surf and Racquet Club.

What to wear

The wedding will be outside on Cumberland Island in June. Although we'll be under the shade of mossy trees, it will probably be warm and humid (lows in the 70's and highs in the upper-80's).

Please dress comfortably. Our wedding planner informs us that you can't go wrong with "resort casual." Seriously: We want you to be comfortable and happy on our big day.

The groom is planning to wear nice dress shoes, while the bride has some adventurous outdoor bridal sandals picked out. Take your cues from them and wear what you like (and don't mind being photographed in).
